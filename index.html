Divvy Bike Usage Patterns: Members vs Casual Riders

This analysis explores the differences in usage patterns between Divvy's annual members and casual riders from December 2023 to December 2024. Using data visualizations, the study highlights trends in ride duration, distance traveled, and time-of-day usage. The goal is to uncover insights that could inform targeted marketing strategies to encourage casual riders to become annual members.
